In the vibrant coastal city of Vlorë, where the Adriatic meets the Ionian Sea, lies a culinary gem that redefines Italian dining — TRE FORCHETTE. More than just a restaurant, it is an experience where flavors, artistry, and hospitality come together in perfect harmony.

Italian Tradition, Elevated

At TRE FORCHETTE, Italian cuisine is not merely served — it is celebrated. Our menu is a curated journey through the rich culinary landscape of Italy, where time-honored recipes meet modern finesse.
